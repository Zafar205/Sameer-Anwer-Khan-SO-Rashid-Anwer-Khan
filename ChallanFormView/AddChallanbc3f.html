Payment Channels for ePay Sindh

Payment through ATM:

  1. Please visit your nearest ATM of 1 Link member bank along with ATM Card.
  2. After PIN verification, select the option of 1Bill (All Invoice) under the option of Bill Payment in the Main Menu.
  3. Enter 19 digits of PSID number and press Ok.
  4. Due Amount against PSID number is automatically fetched and displayed on the ATM screen.
  5. Press Pay/Submit/Finish button.
  6. Message of successful transaction appears on the screen.

Internet/Mobile Banking:

  1. Login to Internet/Mobile Banking.
  2. Select the option of 1Bill (All Invoice) under Payment Options.
  3. Enter 19 digits of PSID number and press Ok.
  4. Due Amount against PSID number is automatically fetched and displayed on the screen.
  5. Press Pay/Submit/Finish button.
  6. Message of successful transaction appears on the screen.

Pay via 1 Link Member Bank Branches:

Please visit the nearest 1 Link Member bank branch along with a print of the PSID and ask the Teller/Cashier to make the payment of levy against the PSID under the option of 1Bill (All Invoice).
